How to correctly measure your waist

To measure your waist, find the bottom of your ribs and the top of your hips, and measure in the middle. This is usually where your belly button is. Ensure that you are relaxed when taking the measurement, i.e. do not hold your stomach in or breath out.

N.B. Do not be tempted to measure the narrowest part of your waist or where the top of your skirt or trousers sit.
